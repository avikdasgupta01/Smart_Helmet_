About

The project involves developing a smart helmet equipped with heat and crash detection systems. Using thermal sensors, the helmet monitors the wearer's temperature, alerting them to potential heat-related risks. Integrated crash sensors detect impacts, triggering automatic emergency notifications to predefined contacts. This enhances rider safety through real-time health monitoring and rapid accident response.
