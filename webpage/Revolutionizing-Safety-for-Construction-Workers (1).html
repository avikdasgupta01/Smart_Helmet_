Defying Heat Stroke with Body

Heat stroke is a serious risk for construction workers, particularly in hot and humid environments. Our
smart helmet is specially designed with body temperature sensors to monitor workers' vital signs, helping
to identify and prevent heat stroke before it occurs.

Falls and impacts are a major source of accidents and injuries on construction sites and bike accidents.
Our smart helmet's crash detection feature is an essential tool for mitigating the risk and severity of these
incidents.
